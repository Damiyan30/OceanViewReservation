Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new reservation?

Go to Add Reservation, fill in the guest details, dates, and room type, then click Confirm Reservation.

Where can I view current bills?

Open the Billing & Invoices page, enter the reservation ID, and click Load Bill.

How do I view an existing booking?

Use the View Reservations page and search using the reservation ID created after saving the booking.

What if the system says unauthorized?

Your session has expired. Return to the login page, sign in again, and continue from the dashboard.
