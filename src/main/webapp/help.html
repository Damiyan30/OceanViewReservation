Frequently Asked Questions

add_circle

How do I add a new reservation?

expand_more

Adding a reservation is simple through the reservation form:

  1. Open the Dashboard.
  2. Choose Add Reservation.
  3. Enter the guest details, dates, and room type.
  4. Click Confirm Reservation.
receipt_long

Where can I view current bills?

expand_more

Open the Billing & Invoices page, enter the reservation ID, and click Load Bill to generate the invoice.

search

How do I view an existing booking?

expand_more

Use the View Reservation page and search using the reservation ID created after saving the booking.

warning

What if the system says unauthorized?

expand_more

Your session has expired. Return to the login page, sign in again, and continue from the dashboard.
